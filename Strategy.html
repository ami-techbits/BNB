BNB Strategy 2034

Shaping the Future of Education — Empowering Future Leaders, One Student at a Time.

Our 10-Year Vision

BNB Learning Center’s Strategy 2034 outlines a bold and innovative roadmap to redefine education, focusing on academic excellence, digital transformation, student well-being, and global citizenship.

Strategic Pillars

High-Quality Education

BNB will enhance its curriculum, promote a reading culture, expand digital learning, and continuously develop teachers to ensure world-class academic excellence.

Happy & Inclusive School

We prioritize student happiness, mental health, and inclusivity, ensuring every child feels safe, valued, and inspired to excel.

Digital Transformation

BNB classrooms will become interactive digital hubs where technology enhances collaboration, critical thinking, and creativity.

Community & Global Partnerships

BNB will strengthen its ties with local businesses and international institutions, fostering global exposure and real-world learning for students.

Financial Sustainability

Through diversified income streams like training, consultancy, and facility rental, BNB will secure its financial future while expanding access to scholarships.

Key Milestones (2024-2034)

  • 2024-2026: Digital classrooms launch, teacher training programs begin.
  • 2027-2029: Infrastructure expansion, global student exchange programs.
  • 2030-2034: Full implementation of holistic excellence model, BNB as a model school in Ethiopia.
