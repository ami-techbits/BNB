Our Story

Founded in 1999 in memory of Dagmawei Yissahek Boncato Bekalo, BNB started with 49 students and has grown into a K-12 institution serving over 2,250 students. We are dedicated to nurturing future leaders with excellence and compassion.

Journey of Excellence

With a 78% pass rate in the National University Entrance Exam—far above the national average— BNB is one of the top-performing schools in Hawassa. Our excellence reflects the dedication of our teachers, modern facilities, and our focus on holistic education.

Our Vision

Our Mission
